Using a computer workstation incorrectly can result in conditions that affect your back, hands, wrists, arms, shoulders and neck. These are known collectively as musculoskeletal disorders (MSDs).

Conditions can range from a temporary soreness of the limbs to more serious disorders such as tendonitis or carpal tunnel syndrome.

The more serious MSD conditions are not always immediately apparent but rather build up over a period of time. Aside from being painful, these conditions can take a long time to recover from.
