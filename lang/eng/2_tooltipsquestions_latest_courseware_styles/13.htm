Reduce
'Reduce' is the first step in the 'Waste Reduction Hierarchy' and aims to prevent wastage from the very beginning. One of the simplest things you can do to help the environment is to reduce the amount of energy, resources and products that you use on a daily basis.
Reuse
'Reuse' is the second step in the 'Waste Reduction Hierarchy', and aims to reuse an object or material again, either for it's original purpose or a similar purpose. It is preferable to recycling as it uses less resources and energy.
Recycle
'Recycle' is the third step in the 'Waste Reduction Hierarchy'. It involves segregating waste for recycle rather than disposal. It is a series of activities by which materials are sorted, processed and used in making new products.
