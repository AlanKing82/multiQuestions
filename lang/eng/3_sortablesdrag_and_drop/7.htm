Conflict of Interest – Reporting Procedure

Where a conflict of interest cannot be avoided or a circumstance has changed and created a conflict, it must be managed properly. Please place your mouse on the steps below and drag into the correct order.

  • Notify your manager who will do an initial assessment and refer to compliance
  • Conflict Register - this includes details of actual/perceived/potential conflict; whether the Xchanging company is regulated and feels a breach has occurred, Compliance review register of conflicts monthly to assess risks and ensure conflicts are discharged appropriately
  • Potential conflict of interest (personal or business) within Xchanging
  • Internal Escalation - dependant of the circumstances compliance may escalate to: Operations Manager, Quality Manager, Regional Quality Manager, Legal Team
  • External Escalation - where necessary compliance will inform the customer and agree a resolution
