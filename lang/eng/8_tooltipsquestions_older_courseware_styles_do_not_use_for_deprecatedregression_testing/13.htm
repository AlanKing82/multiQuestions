There are so many ways you can help conserve water, which not only saves you money, but also protects the planet's resources. Click on the tips below to see how you can save water.

1st Tip
Save on water consumption by installing a simple displacement device in your toilet cistern to reduce its flushing capacity or install a dual-flush device which allows for a short or long flush.
2nd Tip
Have a shower instead of a bath. Showers use 70% less water than a bath.
3rd Tip
Collect rainwater in a barrel to use for your watering purposes in the garden. At work, reuse process water for cooling or less demanding process requirements.
