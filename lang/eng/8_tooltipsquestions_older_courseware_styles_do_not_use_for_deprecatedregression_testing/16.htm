Fire Extinguisher

Use the learning aid ‘PASS’ to remember the steps involved in using an extinguisher.

Interact
Click letters in sequence to view steps.

Pull the pin.

Aim at the base of the fire and stand back six to ten feet.

Squeeze the handle to release the
extinguisher’s contents.

Sweep the hose from side to side at the base of the fire. As the fire dies at the front, move it back slowly until the fire goes out. Only use an extinguisher if safe to do so and you have been trained in its use.
